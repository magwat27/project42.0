Welcome

This website presents the first three chapters of The Wonderful Wizard of Oz by L. Frank Baum. The design uses warm colors, classic typography, and simple navigation to create an easy reading experience.

Readers can move between the home page and each chapter using the navigation links at the top and bottom of every page. Each chapter also includes an image with descriptive alt text, and the layout is designed to keep the text readable and organized.

I chose a classic book-inspired style for this e-book site because it fits the story well and helps make the pages feel calm and easy to read.
